Cream of Spinach Soup

A comforting bowl of soup with the flavor-goodness of spinach, cream and vegetable/chicken broth.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

2 servings

instructions

step 1

Take a large saucepan and place over medium heat, add butter, add the chopped onions to the pan and sauté for 4 minutes or until translucent.

step 2

Now add the potatoes, chicken broth, water, and bouillon cubes. Bring the mixture to a boil, now reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes or until the potatoes are tender. Add spinach and cook for 2 to 4 minutes, until spinach is tender.

step 3

Purée the soup mixture in a blender and return the mixture to the saucepan Over low heat whisk while adding salt, pepper and sour cream until fully incorporated. Garnish with chopped chives, sprinkles of allspice, or a dollop of sour cream and serve.

ingredients

1 Onion, finely chopped2 tbsp Butter2 cup Fresh spinach, chopped3 medium Potatoes (½ kg), peeled and cut into 1-inch cubes

1 and a half cup vegetable broth1 and a half cup Water

2 Chicken or vegetable bouillon cubes, 2 Half and half

1/2 tsp Salt ,1/8 tsp Black Pepper

3/4 Sour cream

A pinch of Ground all-spice

3/4 cup Chopped chives for garnish
