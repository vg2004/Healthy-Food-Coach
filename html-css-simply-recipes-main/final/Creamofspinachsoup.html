Spinach and Banana Pancakes

A perfect combination of healthy and tasty, spinach and banana pancake can be your next go-to breakfast for all things nutritionally-rich. A great recipe curated by the ITM Institute of Hotel Management.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

2 servings

instructions

step 1

Add the oats to a high speed blender or food processor and blend until broken down into a flour like consistency.

step 2

Add the milk, spinach, banana, cinnamon, egg and vanilla and blend until the spinach is completely broken down and the batter is smooth.

step 3

Rest the batter for 10 minutes. Heat up a non stick pan and add butter/oil to it. Pour the pancake batter with a ladle and slightly brown on both sides. Serve with a side of nut butter or fresh fruits.

ingredients

240 gms rolled oats

150 ml milk

3200 gram spinach

1 Ripe banana 1 Egg

1/2 tsp cinnamon powder

1/2 tsp vanilla extract

1 tsp baking powder
