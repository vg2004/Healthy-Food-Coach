Soya Khichdi

prep time

20 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

2 servings

instructions

step 1

To begin with, boil water in a large bottomed pan, add soya beans, rice along with tomatoes and green chillies. Cook until the rice and beans are soft and mushy.

step 2

Heat oil/ghee in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter. After it, add chopped onion and ginger-garlic paste, and saut√© for a minute or two

step 3

Add turmeric, yogurt, salt to taste, peas followed by cooked rice and beans. Stirring constantly for at least 10 minutes, add water as per the requirement and then add chopped coriander leaves.

ingredients

1 cup Soya bean, soaked1 1/2 cup Rice, soaked2 Green chillies2 Tomato, chopped1 Large onion, chopped1 tbsp Ginger-garlic paste1 tbsp Oil

1 tsp Haldi

1 tsp Jeera

2-3 tsp Fresh coriander leaves, chopped

Salt to taste

1/2 cup Yogurt

1/4 cup Green peas
